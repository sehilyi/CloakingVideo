• Step 1

    Turn off any Ad Block Extensions

    If you are using in your Chrome browser

  • Step 2

    Play any YouTube video

    that you want to pretend to watch

  • Step 3

    Paste URL of another YouTube video

    that you want to watch subtly

  • Step 4

    Click Watch Video! button

    in the CloakingVideo popup view

  • Done !

    Watch subtly!

    The entered YouTube video will be shown as a video advertisement on the right side of the YouTube site

This Chrome extension is based on a research paper.
