CloakingVideo

Watch YouTube Videos Without Being Noticed By Nearby People!

HOW TO USE

  1. In YouTube.com, play any YouTube video (perhaps, a lecture video) that you pretend to watch.
  2. Paste an URL of another YouTube video (either an entire URL or the right-most part of it) that you want to watch subtly.
  3. Click the Watch Video! Button.

Example URL:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FEsFXsPCVOo

This extension is based on a research paper.
